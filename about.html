Comapny Background

FarjiyaTech was founded in 2022 by Farjiya, an Algonquin College graduate in Interactive Media Design. The company was established with the goal of helping businesses establish a strong online presence and leverage the power of digital marketing. Farjiya's passion for digital media started early on, and she pursued her interests by studying Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College. During her studies, she gained valuable experience in website design, UX design, and digital marketing. She recognized the potential of these areas and decided to turn her passion into a career.

After graduating from Algonquin College, Farjiya founded FarjiyaTech to help businesses harness the power of digital media. The company started with just a few clients, but it quickly grew as Farjiya's reputation for high-quality work spread. As the company expanded, Farjiya brought on a team of talented designers, developers, and digital marketers who shared her vision and passion for digital media. Together, they worked to create unique and effective solutions for their clients, ranging from small startups to large corporations.

Today, FarjiyaTech is a leading provider of website design, logo design, UX design, and digital marketing services. The company has helped numerous businesses establish a strong online presence and grow their brand through effective digital marketing strategies. At FarjiyaTech, we are committed to providing our clients with the highest quality service and results. We are proud of our company history and the positive impact we have made on our clients' businesses. We look forward to continuing to help businesses achieve their digital goals in the years to come.

Farjiya BentaM

Farjiya is an immigrant from Bangladesh who came to Canada with a dream of becoming a web developer. She was determined to pursue her passion for digital media and create websites that are user-friendly, secure, and accessible by everyone. Despite facing many challenges as an immigrant, Farjiya worked hard to achieve her goals. She enrolled in Algonquin College's Interactive Media Design program, where she gained valuable experience in website design, UX design, and digital marketing.

During her studies, Farjiya developed a deep understanding of the importance of putting the client's needs first. She recognized that every business is unique and requires a tailored solution that meets their specific needs. This led her to establish FarjiyaTech, where she could provide high-quality web development and digital marketing services that prioritize the client's needs and goals.

Farjiya has a strong work ethic and is committed to delivering the best work possible. She understands the value of trust and transparency in building strong relationships with her clients. That's why she ensures that her clients are involved in the development process every step of the way, from the initial planning stages to the final product. Farjiya's dedication to her clients and her passion for digital media have earned her a reputation for delivering exceptional results. She is proud to have helped many businesses establish a strong online presence and achieve their digital goals through FarjiyaTech's services.

At FarjiyaTech, we continue to uphold Farjiya's values and commitment to excellence in all our work. We are proud to have her as our founder and to continue providing high-quality web development and digital marketing services to our clients.
