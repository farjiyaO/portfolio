Company Background

FarjiyaTech was founded in 2022 by Farjiya, an Algonquin College graduate in Interactive Media Design. The company was established with the goal of helping businesses establish a strong online presence and leverage the power of digital marketing.

Today, FarjiyaTech is a leading provider of website design, logo design, UX design, and digital marketing services. The company has helped numerous businesses establish a strong online presence and grow their brand through effective digital marketing strategies.

Mission:

Earn your trust and help you thrive

Vision:

Building a secure, stable and modern website for your business.

Farjiya BentaM

Farjiya is an immigrant from Bangladesh who came to Canada with a dream of becoming a web developer. She was determined to pursue her passion for digital media and create websites that are user-friendly, secure, and accessible by everyone.

As the company expanded, Farjiya brought on a team of talented designers, developers, and digital marketers who shared her vision and passion for digital media. Together, they worked to create unique and effective solutions for their clients, ranging from small startups to large corporations.
